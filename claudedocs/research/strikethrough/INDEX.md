## Key Concepts

- **Sous Rature**: Derrida's "under erasure" technique (text crossed out but still readable)
- **X-Mark Detection**: Pattern-based detection of crossing marks
- **Multi-Layer Approach**: Statistical → Visual → OCR pipeline
- **Validation**: Real PDF testing with manually verified ground truth
